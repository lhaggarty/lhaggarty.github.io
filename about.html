Most people stop watching an online video before it ends

Approximately 50% of viewers stop watching a video after the first quarter (Wistia). Lectures, shows, news... Ensure the viewer doesn't miss the important bits by shortening, joining, and mashing up online video. Share the video snippet with a friend or in a presentation.

Feature films would be hours long without video editing. The editing of a movie is paramount to its entertainment value. An unedited video is much less captivating than a edited video. Video editing is currently done locally on your computer. A video is uploaded onto the Internet after it is edited, once a video is put online the editing stops. This begs the question of why don't we edit video online? Previously it wasn't simple, easy, or effective.
