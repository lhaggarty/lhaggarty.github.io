BERST

Role

Wearer of Many Hats (UX, Design, Frontend Dev, Information Architecture, Business Dev)

Description

Berst is a news aggregator, which through the use of an array of natural language processing techniques, summarizes popular news stories and evaluates the quality or bias of each source.

I started this project based on the simple notion that the news is often presented in a fashion that's much more complex than it ought to be, and that bias could generally be identified by comparing each story on a particular topic and looking for slight changes in word choice and grammatical structure (e.g., the word shooter vs the word terrorist).

ARDUINO SYNTHESIZER

Description

The synthesizer is part of my exploration into the space of physical computing. It functions as part arpeggiator part step-sequencer. It features three input modes; two photoresistors, which operate independently as additive and subtractive modules, and a dial which controls tempo. The first iteration features a hard-coded note pattern. The goal of this project was to experiment and determine whether constructing a full fledged synthesizer was within reason. I was quite happy with the result, and am currently working to design a more feature laden second version.

NEWS & SOCIAL MEDIA SENTIMENT ELECTORAL PREDICTION

Description

My major research project for my graduate studies. For this I'm assessing the strategic utility of predictive models based on news and social media sentiment. There has been considerable research into the development of these models, but I want to explore their potential applications.

I'll be reviewing a number of case studies from various elections around the world to review the efficacy of their proposed models, as well as investigating their place within the political landscape of each nation.
